# Persona Deck

Use this reference when customizing a debate.

## Default Personas

- Frontier Optimist: maximizes upside, second-order adoption, and new behaviors.
- Skeptical Engineer: tests reliability, hidden complexity, data quality, evals, and deployment pain.
- Market Realist: asks who pays, who switches, what budget moves, and which channel wins.
- China Contextualizer: localizes for WeChat, Douyin, enterprise procurement, compliance, language, and platform habits.
- Trust Editor: watches privacy, safety, labor impact, copyright, provenance, and reputational risk.
- Audience Editor: converts the winning argument into a watchable short-form package.

## Optional Personas

- Open-Source Maximalist: asks whether community speed beats closed labs.
- Enterprise Buyer: cares about procurement, integration, security review, and ROI.
- Creator Economist: evaluates whether the idea creates status, income, or audience growth.
- Product Minimalist: removes features until the core behavior is unmistakable.
- Regulator: asks what becomes illegal, restricted, or institutionally risky.

## Scorecard

Rate 1-5:

- Novelty: would a smart AI viewer feel a new thought?
- Evidence: can the claim survive source checks?
- Visual potential: can it be shown, not only said?
- Controversy: will thoughtful people disagree?
- Creator fit: does it match the user's voice and audience?

## Synthesis Rule

Pick the argument with the best product of novelty, evidence, and visual potential. Controversy is useful only when the claim remains defensible.
